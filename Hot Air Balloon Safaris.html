Hot Air Balloon Safari

Hot air balloon flights usually depart at sunrise, so be prepared for a very early wake-up call! A representative from your chosen company will collect you at your hotel and transfer you to the launch site. Once you arrive, you’ll receive a safety briefing on land as your pilot fires up the balloon. Then, it’s time to board! Your group will head into the basket, and — just like that — the balloon will begin its gentle ascent. You’ll be airborne before you know it, just in time to catch the spectacular sunrise from your bird’s-eye view vantage point. Hovering above the treetops with the never-ending plains of the savannah in front of you, you’ll drift through the sky for an hour, keeping an eye out for wildebeests, elephants, lions, and more in their natural habitat below. The best part? The experience doesn’t end when you land! Back on solid ground, you’ll be treated to a freshly-prepared champagne breakfast — complete with linens and fine china — outside in the African bush.

Best destinations for a hot air balloon safari

Kenya

Kenya’s renowned national parks attract thousands of wildlife-seeking travellers every year, but only a handful have seen them from the sky. Spot the Big Five from a unique vantage point before enjoying a spectacular champagne breakfast served in the bush

Tanzania

Rise with the morning sun and float gracefully over Tanzania’s most iconic national park, the Serengeti, while your pilot points out a diverse range of wildlife below, from elephants to giraffes, zebras, lions, and more.

South Africa
